What is a Unicorn?

In economics, a unicorn is a startup company with a value of over 1 billion euros, which is privately owned and not listed on a stock exchange. The term was first published in 2013 and was coined by venture capitalist Aileen Lee, who chose the mythical animal to symbolize the statistical rarity of such successful ventures.
