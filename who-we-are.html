Founder

Hatice Tavlı

Founder & Head Engineer – Smart Robotics and Systems

Hatice Tavli is a Turkish-German applied mathematician and the visionary founder of IVDY Group. She leads the development of AI-powered digital assistants that reduce misdiagnosis and enhance diagnostic efficiency in healthcare. Specializing in non-smooth and non-convex optimization, deep learning, komplex dynamical systems, inverse Problems und Quantenphysics, Hatice designs intelligent systems that transform complex data into real-time, actionable insights. Hatice leads IVDY’s mission to bridge advanced mathematics with practical solutions, uniting applied mathematicians, engineers and data scientists to tackle some of the most complex challenges to moving society forward and solving many of the world’s most pressing challenges.
